Summary

Currently, I'm a double PhD candidate in the Postgraduate Program in Ecology at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS - Brazil) and at the Groningen Institute for Evolutionary Life Sciences at the University of Groningen (RUG - Netherlands). In addition, I also work as a project ecologist at the company AEROGEO Aerofotogrametria, Geoprocessamento e Engenharia LTDA. I hold a master's in ecology and a bachelor's in biological sciences from UFRGS. During my undergraduate course, I participated in an exchange program where I studied at the University of Aberdeen as part of the Science without Borders program, receiving a scholarship from CNPq.

Main Skills

  • Languages: Portugues (native), English (fluent) and Spanish (intermediate)
  • Field work & Laboratory: Insects sampling (sweep nets, pitfall traps, beat sheet), Insect Identification key, Light Microscopy
  • Programming & Statistics: R (lm4, ape, phytools, tydiverse, vegan, raster, letsR, ggplot2), html
  • Others: Interpersonal skills; Multicultural environments; Presentation and review of scientific papers; Organizational skills; Scientific writing
