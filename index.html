Curriculum vitae

Currently, I'm a double PhD candidate in the Postgraduate Program in Ecology at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS - Brazil) and at the Groningen Institute for Evolutionary Life Sciences at the University of Groningen (RUG - Netherlands). In my thesis, I’m studying patterns and evolutionary consequences of galling insects and its host plants interactions. In addition, I also work as a project ecologist at the company AEROGEO Aerofotogrametria, Geoprocessamento e Engenharia LTDA., where I have already identified more than 11 thousand plants for the formulation of a water piping project in the city where I live, evaluating possible environmental impacts to reduce them. I hold a master's in ecology and a bachelor's in biological sciences from UFRGS. During my undergraduate course, I participated in an exchange program where I studied at the University of Aberdeen for a year as part of the Science without Borders program, receiving a scholarship from CNPq.
